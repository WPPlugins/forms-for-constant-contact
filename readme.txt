== Description ==

An easy way to place a constant contact forms on your Wordpress site.

###What you get when you use the constant contact forms

*  Place a constant contact forms with a simple shortcode [constant_contact_forms id="%postId%"]
*  Put constant contact forms on any post or page.
*  constant contact forms headline
*  constant contact forms background color
*  constant contact forms message
*  constant contact forms button
*  Unlimited constant contact forms

###Add constant contact forms to any post or page

Whether you are writing a post about your company or another company, you can include a well formatted constant contact forms.

###Quick Start Guide

* [constant contact forms quick start guide at thinklandingpages.com](http://www.thinklandingpages.com/constant-contact-forms/)

###Unlimited constant contact forms

Add as many constant contact forms to your site as you like.  The constant contact forms lets you create forms and save them for when ever you are ready to use them.


== Installation ==

1. Upload constant-contact-forms to the /wp-content/constant contact formss/ directory
1. Activate the constant contact forms through the 'Constant Contact Formss' menu in WordPress
1. Click **Constant Contact Forms** on the admin menu to enable and set your options.


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I have to know how to program or design? =

No.  The constant contact forms does the programming and design.

= Is there a limit to the number of constant contact forms I can create? =
You can create an unlimited number of GetResponse forms.

= Can the constant contact forms be put on my homepage? =
Yes, you can put constant contact forms on your homepage by using the shortcode on you homepage.

= Can I put constant contact forms on any post or page? =
Yes, use the constant contact forms shortcode, [constant_contact_forms id="%postId%"] on your pages and posts.
